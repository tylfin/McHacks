About Clefer

Clefer is a more clever way to make music. It's a music parser and computer-generated random song creator.

It was designed ontop of the Music21 toolkit, Zen network module and the python programming language. By parsing the notes, rests, chords and offsets of the music given, Clefer creates a new song based on the natural probabilities that exist in music. Clefer is giving the bass clef new meaning.

To use Clefer, simply upload a .mid file what's returned is the computer-generated song.
